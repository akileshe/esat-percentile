ESAT Percentile Calculator

Choose up to three modules and enter three scaled scores (1.0–9.0) to see percentile estimates for UK, Overseas, China and an overall group.
Percentiles are estimated by linear interpolation using ESAT Technical Report 2024–25 Tables 8 & 9. Extrapolation is used below the 25th and above the 90th percentile anchor points. The “Overall” group uses the average of the UK, Overseas and China percentile anchor scores.
